Nature Retreat Odyssey

Tour details

Embark on an 11-day relaxation tour in Sri Lanka. Explore Kandy’s cultural heritage, Ella’s natural beauty, Yala’s wildlife, and the serene beaches of Mirissa and Bentota. Highlights include Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, Nine Arch Bridge, Yala National Park, and whale watching in Mirissa. A perfect blend of nature, wildlife, and relaxation awaits you.

Program

Day 1
Bandaranaike International Airport > Kandy 122 KM

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, our Guide will warmly welcome you at the airport and you will be brought to your accommodation at Kandy

On the way to Kandy you can visit,

  • Visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage.
  • Herbal & Spice garden

Proceed to Kandy. Check in to hotel & stay relax. Here you can visit

  • Temple of the tooth relic
  • Gem Museum & Batik Gallery
  • Cultural dance show

Overnight stay @Kandy (Suggestions – 5 star – Mahaweli reach hotel , 4star – Topaz Hotel )

Day 2
Kandy > Ella 137 KM

Check out from the hotel and Proceed to Ella. On the way to Ella you can visit,

  • Tea Garden & tea Factory
  • Ramboda waterfall

If you like to train ride you can go to Nuwara eliya to Ella by train. Or else you can go by car.

Check-in to hotel & stay relax.

  • Evening walk in Ella Town

Overnight stay @Ella (Suggestions – 4 star – Hide Ella, 98 Acres )

Ella
