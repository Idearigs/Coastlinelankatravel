Lanka Adventure Trail

Tour overview

Experience the best of Sri Lanka in an 8-day tour. Start in Negombo, explore Kandy’s cultural treasures, enjoy the serene beauty of Nuwara Eliya, and the stunning views of Ella. Experience a safari in Uduwalawa and relax on the beaches of Bentota. Highlights include Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, Nine Arch Bridge, and Galle Fort.

Program

Day 1
Airport > Negambo | 7.7kM | 17min

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport you will be brought to your accomodation in Negombo. Relax and go to the beach or let us guide you to the Fish market

Overnight stay at negambo
( 4 star – Amagi aria or similar, 5 star – Sentido Heritance Negambo or similar )

Day 2
Negombo > kandy | 115KM | 2Hr 41min

On your way to Kandy and in Kandy there are a number of highlights to discover, for example: Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, spices garden, gem museum, Temple of the Tooth, cultural dance and a batik factory. Pick your favorites or see them all!

Overnight stay at Kandy ( 4 star – Cinnamon Citadel or similar, 5 star – Mahaweli Reach or similar )

Day 3
Kandy > Nuwaraeliya | 76KM | 2Hr 31min

The next location during your tour is the beautiful city Nuwara Eliya and it’s surroundings. Here, in the Sri Lankan hills you will be able to visit a Tea factory, Gregory Lake and Victoria Park

Overnight stay at NuwaraEliya ( 4 star – Araliya Red or similar, 5 star – Araliya Green City or similar )

Day 4
Nuwara Eliya > Ella | 54KM | 1Hr 48min

You will be impressed by the beauty of Sri Lanka, while travelling by train from Kandy to Ella. After the train trip, you will go hiking in National Park Horton Plains and enjoy the stunning view of World’s End.

Overnight stay at Ella ( 4 star – Hide Ella or similar, 98 Acress or Similar )

Day 5
Ella

The highlights of day 5 are the Nine Arch Bridge, Little Adam’s Peak and Rawana Fall.

Overnight stay at Ella ( 4 star – Hide Ella or similar, 98 Acress or Similar )

Day 6
Ella > Udawalawa | 90KM | 1Hr 55min

After two days of walking, it’s time to let the 4-wheel drive do the work for you in order to enjoy the safari at Uduwalawa National Park How many elephants will you spot??

Overnight stay at Udawalawa ( 4 star – Centauria Wild / Grand Udawalawe or Similar )

Day 7
Udawalawa > Bentota | 182KM | 2Hr 43min

On your way to Bentota, you will visit Galle Fort and a Turtle Hatchery. In Bentota, you can relax and enjoy a cocktail at the beach of Bentota, where you will also stay for the night.

Overnight stay at Udawalawa ( 4 star – Pandanus beach resort & spa or Similar, 5 star – Sheraton kosgoda or similar )

Day 8
Bentota > Airport | 109KM | 1Hr 44min

Today, we will drop you off at the airport (or at another location).
