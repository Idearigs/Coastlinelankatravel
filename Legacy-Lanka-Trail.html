Legacy Lanka Trail

Tour overview

Immerse yourself in Sri Lanka's culture and heritage on a 7-day tour. Explore the ancient city of Sigiriya, relax on Trincomalee's beaches, experience Kandy's vibrant culture, and discover Colombo's urban charm. Highlights include Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, Dambulla Cave Temple, and Lion Rock. A captivating journey through Sri Lanka's rich history and diverse landscapes awaits you.

Itinerary

Day 1
Pick Up from Bandaranaike International Airport > Sigiriya | 146 KM | 3Hours and 12 Minutes

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, our Guide will warmly welcome you at the airport and you will be brought to your accommodation at Sigiriya.

On the way to Sigiriya you can visit,

  • Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage
  • Dambulla Cave Temple

After reach to sigiriya check-in to hotel & stay relax. In the evening you can visit,

  • Herbal & Ayurveda spa

Overnight stay at Sigiriya (Suggestions 5 star- Cinnamon Lodge, 4 star- Cinnamon Village)

Day 2
Sigiriya

Here you can visit,

  • Lion Rock
  • Traditional & Village Tour
  • Safari at Minneriya National Park

Overnight stay at Sigiriya (Suggestions 5 star- Cinnamon Lodge, 4 star- Cinnamon Village)

Day 3
Sigiriya > Trincomalee | 100 KM | 1Hour and 56 Minutes

Here you can check out from the hotel & proceed to Trincomalee. Here you can visit,

  • Nilaweli Beach
  • Fort Fredrick
  • Thirukoneshwaram Temple
  • Stay relax at the beach

Overnight stay at Sigiriya (Suggestions 4 star- Trinco Blu by Cinnamon, Amaranthe bay )

Day 4
Trincomalee > Kandy | 179 KM | 4Hours and 00 Minutes

Here you can proceed to Kandy. On the way to Kandy you can visit,

  • Herbal & spice garden

Reach to Kandy & check-in to Hotel.

  • Evening visit Cultural dance show

Overnight stay at Kandy (Suggestions – 5 star - Mahaweli reach hotel , 4star – Topaz Hotel )

Day 5
Kandy

Here you can visit ,

  • Kandy view point
  • Royal botanical garden
  • Gem museum & Batik Gallery

Overnight stay at Kandy (Suggestions – 5 star - Mahaweli reach hotel , 4star – Topaz Hotel )

Day 6
Kandy > Colombo | 122 KM | 2Hours and 49 Minutes

Here you can visit,

  • City tour of Colombo with Galle face green, Old parliament building, Gangaramaya temple, Shopping at One Galle face shopping mall, Pettah Market

Overnight stay @ Colombo (Suggestions 5 star – Cinnamon Grand, 4 star- Radisson Colombo)

Day 7
Colombo > Bandaranayke International Airport 32KM | 0Hours and 40Minutes

Here you can check out from the hotel & you will be transfer to Bandaranayke International Airport according to your flight timings.

End of the tour!!!
