Program

Day 1
Negombo

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport you will be brought to your accomodation in Negombo. Relax and go to the beach or let us guide you to the Fish market

Day 2
Kandy

On your way to Kandy we visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage where you can breed the baby elephants and see the elephants bathing. In Kandy there are a number of highlights to discover, for example: spices garden, gem museum, Peradeniya Botanical Garden, cultural dance and local market. Pick your favorites or see them all!

Day 3 & 4
Nuwara Eliya

The next location during your tour is the beautiful city Nuwara Eliya and it’s surroundings. Here, in the Sri Lankan hills you will be able to visit a Tea factory, Ramnboda water fall, Gregory Lake and Victoria Park On day 4, you can either visit the beautiful flora and fauna of the Horton Plains National Park or visit Lovers Leaf Waterfall, Haggala Botanical Garden and do a city tour in Nuwara Eliya.

Day 5 & 6
Ella

We travel from Kandy to Ella by train. The traintrack is right through the mountains area and will be an unforgettable experience for you. Ella is a very little town with stunning panoramical views. The highlights of day 6 are the Nine Arch Bridge, Little Adam’s Peak and Rawana Fall. Ella is located 1.500 metres above sealevel, so the climate is very pleasant

Day 7
Yala

Have you brought your binoculars? Because, today you have the change to spot the leopard, elephants and numerous other animals during a Safari in Yala National Park.

Day 8
Udawalawa

Udawalawa National Park is famous for its numerous amounts of elephants. During the late afternoon safari many elephants and beautiful birds can be spotted. How many will you count?

Day 9
Galle

On day 9 you will stay at an accomdation near the beach in Galle and have the opportunity to visit for instance Galle Fort, Martin Wickramasinghe Museum and the Stick Fishermen. In the evening, you can have a romantic dinner at the beach.

Day 10 & 11
Bentota

In Bentota, you will visit a turtle hatchery and we will do a River Safari on the Madu River. after that, you can relax and enjoy a cocktail at the beach of Bentota, where you will also stay for the night. On day 11, it’s time for a Beach day. Want some action? There are plenty of water sports options!

Day 12
Colombo

Today, we will show you our capital city during a Colombo city tour. In the shopping street, there are plenty of shopping options where you can buy souvernirs.

Day 13
Colombo

This will be your last day of your holiday. We will drop you off at the airport. It has been a pleasure to guide you through our beautiful country
