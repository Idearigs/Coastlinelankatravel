Blissful Union Journey

Tour details

Embark on a 13-day tour of Sri Lanka, starting in Negombo, exploring the heritage city of Anuradhapura, Kandy, the natural beauty of Nuwara Eliya and Ella, the wildlife of Yala and Udawalawa, the colonial charm of Galle, and the beaches of Bentota. End your journey with a city tour of Colombo. A perfect blend of culture, nature, and relaxation.

Itinerary

Day 1
Airport > Negombo | 7.7KM | 17min

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport you will be brought to your accommodation in Negombo. Relax and go to the beach or let us guide you to the Fish market

Overnight stay at Negombo
( -4 star Amagi aria , 5 star – Sentido , Heritance Negombo )

Day 2
Negombo > Kandy | 114 KM | 2Hr 24min

On your way to Kandy we visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage where you can breed the baby elephants and see the elephants bathing. In Kandy there are a number of highlights to discover, Pick your favorites or see them all!

  • Spice Garden
  • Gem Museum
  • Peradeniya Botanical Garden
  • Cultural dance
  • Local Market

Overnight stay at Kandy ( 4 star – Cinnamon Citadel or similar, 5 star – Mahaweli Reach or similar )

Day 3
Kandy > Nuwara Eliya | 76 KM | 2Hr 24min

The next location during your tour is the beautiful city Nuwara Eliya and it's surroundings. Here, in the Sri Lankan hills you will be able to visit ,

  • Tea factory
  • Ramboda water fall
  • Gregory Lake
  • Victoria Park

Proceed

Overnight stay at Nuwara eliya ( 4 star – Araliya red or similar, 5 star – Araliya Green city or similar )

Day 4
Nuwara Eliya

On day 4, you can visit ,

  • Horton Plains
  • Haggala Botanical Garden

Overnight stay at Nuwara eliya ( 4 star – Araliya red or similar, 5 star – Araliya Green city or similar )

Day 5
Nuwara Eliya > Ella | 55kM | 1Hr 46min

We travel from Nuwara Eliya to Ella by train. The traintrack is right through the mountains area and will be an unforgettable experience for you. Ella is a very little town with stunning panoramical views.

Overnight stay at Ella ( 4 star – Hide Ella or similar, 98 Acress or Similar )

Day 6
Ella

Ella is located 1.500 metres above sea level, so the climate is very pleasant .The highlights of day 6 are ,

  • Nine Arch Bridge
  • Rawana Water Falls

Overnight stay at Ella ( 4 star – Hide Ella / 98 Acress or Similar )

Day 7
Ella > Yala | 58KM | 1Hr 14min

Have you brought your binoculars? Because, today you have the change to spot the leopard, elephants and numerous other animals during a Safari in Yala National Park.

Overnight stay at Yala ( 4 star – Chaarya Resort & Spa or similar or Cinnamon Wild or Similar )

Day 8
Yala > Udawalawa | 64KM | 1Hr 18min

Udawalawa National Park is famous for its numerous amounts of elephants. During the late afternoon safari many elephants and beautiful birds can be spotted. How many will you count?

Overnight stay at udawalawa ( 4 star – Centauria Wild / Grand Udawalawe or Similar )

Day 9
Udawalawa > Galle | 127KM | 1Hr 59min

On day 9 you will stay at an accommodation near the beach in Galle and have the opportunity to visit ,

  • Galle Fort
  • Marting Wickramasinghe
  • Stick Fisherman

Overnight stay at Galle ( 4 star – Tamarind Hill or Similar, 5 star – Radisson Galle or similar )

Day 10
Galle > Bentota | 70KM | 1Hr 11min

In Bentota, you will visit ,

  • Turtle Hatchery
  • River Safari

After that, you can relax and enjoy a cocktail at the beach of Bentota, where you will also stay for the night.

Overnight stay at Bentota ( 4 star – Pananus beach resort & spa or Similar, 5 star – Sheraton kosgoda or similar )

Day 11
Bentota

On day 11, it's time for a Beach day. Want some action? There are plenty of water sports options!

Overnight stay at Bentota (4 star – Pananus beach resort & spa or Similar, 5 star – Sheraton kosgoda or similar )

Day 12
Bentota > Colombo | 86KM | 1Hr 31min

Today, we will show you our capital city during a Colombo city tour. In the shopping street, there are plenty of shopping options where you can buy souvernirs.

Overnight stay at Colombo (4 star – Cinnamon Red or Similar, 5 star – Cinnamon Grand or similar )

Day 13
Colombo > Airport | 35 KM | 35min

This will be your last day of your holiday. We will drop you off at the airport. It has been a pleasure to guide you through our beautiful country
