Love Lanka Retreat

Tour overview

Experience the romance of Sri Lanka on a 6-day honeymoon tour. Explore Kandy’s cultural treasures, enjoy the serene beauty of Nuwara Eliya, and relax on the beaches of Bentota. Highlights include Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, Temple of the Tooth, tea gardens, and water sports. A perfect blend of adventure and relaxation for newlyweds.

Itinerary

Day 1
Pick Up from Bandaranaike International Airport > Kandy 122 KM | 2Hours and 49 Minutes

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, our Guide will warmly welcome you at the airport and you will be brought to your accommodation at Kandy.

On the way to Kandy you can visit,

  • Visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage.
  • Herbal & Spice garden

Proceed to Kandy. Check in to hotel & stay relax. In the evening visit

  • Temple of the tooth relic
  • Cultural dance show

Overnight stay @Kandy (Suggestions – 5 star – Mahaweli reach hotel , 4star – Topaz Hotel )

Day 2
Kandy > Nuwara Eliya 76 KM | 2Hours and 20 Minutes

Here you can visit

  • Gem Museum & Batik Gallery

Check out from the hotel and Proceed to Nuwara Eliya. On the way to Nuwara Eliya you can visit,

  • Tea Garden & tea Factory
  • Ramboda waterfall
  • Hanuman Temple

Proceed to Nuwara Eliya. Here you can visit,

  • Tea Garden & tea Factory
  • Seetha Amman Temple

Overnight stay @Nuwara Eliya (Suggestions – 5 star - Araliya Green city, 4star – Araliya Red)

Day 3
Nuwara Eliya

Here you can visit,

  • Victoria park
  • Gregory Lake

Overnight stay @Nuwara Eliya (Suggestions – 5 star - Araliya Green city, 4star – Araliya Red)

Day 4
Nuwara Eliya > Bentota 210 KM | 5 Hours and 16 Minutes

Checkout from the hotel & proceed to Bentota. Here you can visit,

  • Turtle hatchery
  • Madhu river boat Safari

Overnight stay at Bentota (Suggestions – 5 star – Sheraton kosgoda, 4 star – Pandanus beach hotel)

Day 5
Bentota

Here you can do,

  • Water sports (Jet Ski ride, Banana boat ride, etc.)
  • Stay relax at the beach

Overnight stay @Bentota (Suggestions – 5 star – Sheraton kosgoda, 4 star – Pandanus beach hotel)

Day 6
Bentota > Bandaranayke International Airport 108KM | 1Hour and 30Minutes

Here you can check out from the hotel & you will be transfer to Bandaranayke International Airport according to your flight timings.

End of the tour!!!
