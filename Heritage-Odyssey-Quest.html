Heritage Odyssey Quest

Tour overview

Experience the wonders of Sri Lanka in an 11-day tour. From the beaches of Negombo and Trincomalee, the cultural heritage of Anuradhapura and Kandy, the natural beauty of Ella, to the colonial charm of Galle. Highlights include Lion’s Rock, Minneriya National Park, and Nine Arch Bridge. A journey of discovery and relaxation awaits you.

Program

Day 1
Negombo

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport you will be brought to your accomodation in Negombo. You can do whatever you like. For instance, relax and go to the beach or let us guide you to the Fish market

Day 2
Anuradhapura

The second day we will head to World Heritage city Anuradhapura and visit a number of cultural attractions, like: Ruwanwelisay, Sri Maha Bodhi, Abhayagiri Stupa, Mihintale, Twin Ponds, Isurumuniya.

Day 3 & 4
Trincomalee

Day 3: Trincomalee is famous for the relaxed atmosphere. Therefore, you will go to the Hot water wells

Day 4: Beach.

Both nights you will stay at an accomodation in Trincomalee

Day 5 & 6
Habarana

We will discover the fascinating wildlife of Minneriya National Park during a safari tour on day 5.

On day 4, we go to the famous Lion’s Rock (Sigiriya), after which you can visit the Cultural village tour and bring a visit to Polonnaruwa.

Both nights you will stay at an accomodation in Habarana.

Day 7 & 8
Kandy

While we are driving to Kandy and in Kandy, there are a number of highlights to discover, for example: Dambulla cave temple, spices garden, gem museum, Temple of the Tooth, cultural dance and local market. Pick your favorites or see them all!We will discover the fascinating wildlife of Minneriya National Park during a safari tour on day 5.

On Day 8, you can relax in the Peradeniya Botanical Gardens and shop Batiks at the local market.

Day 9
Ella

The highlights of day 8 are the Nine Arch Bridge, Little Adam’s Peak and Rawana Fall. In short, a fantastic day in nature. After the sightseeing you will go to your accomodation in Ella and will enjoy the evening.

Day 10
Galle

On day 10 you will stay at an accomdation near the beach in Galle and have the opportunity to visit for instance Galle Fort, Martin Wickramasinghe Museum and the Stick Fishermen

Day 11
Galle

Today, we will drop you off at the airport (or at another location).
