Romantic Isle Escape

Tour details

Embark on an enchanting 8-day journey across the diverse landscapes of Sri Lanka. Start in the historic city of Kandy, visiting the revered Temple of the Tooth and the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage. Journey through the lush tea gardens of Nuwara Eliya, and enjoy the serene beauty of Gregory Lake. Explore the charming town of Ella, with its iconic Nine Arch Bridge and stunning views from Little Adam's Peak. Unwind on the golden beaches of Bentota, indulge in water sports, and visit a turtle hatchery. Conclude your tour in the bustling city of Colombo, soaking in its vibrant culture and landmarks. Your memorable Sri Lankan adventure ends as you depart from Bandaranaike International Airport. Enjoy the journey!
