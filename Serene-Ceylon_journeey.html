Serene Ceylon Journey

Tour overview

Experience the beauty of Sri Lanka in an 8-day tour. Explore Kandy's rich culture, Nuwara Eliya's tea gardens, Ella's stunning views, and Bentota's water sports. Enjoy Colombo's city life before your departure. Highlights include Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, Temple of the Tooth, Seetha Amman Temple, and more. A journey of breathtaking sights and unforgettable experiences.

Itinerary

Day 1
Pick Up from Bandaranaike International Airport > Kandy 122 KM | 2Hours and 49 Minutes

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, our Guide will warmly welcome you at the airport and you will be brought to your accommodation at Kandy

On the way to Kandy you can visit,

  • Visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage.
  • Herbal & Spice garden

Proceed to Kandy. Check in to hotel & stay relax. In the evening visit,

  • Temple of the tooth relic

Overnight stay at Kandy (Suggestions – 5 star – Mahaweli reach hotel , 4star – Topaz Hotel )

Day 2
Kandy

Here you can visit,

  • Kandy View point
  • Royal Botanical garden
  • Gem Museum & Batik Gallery
  • Giant Buddha statue
  • Cultural dance show

Overnight stay at Kandy (Suggestions – 5 star – Mahaweli reach hotel , 4star – Topaz Hotel )

Day 3
Kandy > Nuwara Eliya 76 KM | 2Hours and 20 Minutes

After your breakfast you can check out from the hotel and Proceed to Nuwara Eliya. On the way to Nuwara Eliya you can visit,

  • Tea Garden & tea Factory
  • Ramboda waterfall
  • Hanuman Temple

Proceed to Nuwara Eliya. Here you can visit,

  • Seetha Amman Temple
  • Gregory Lake

Overnight stay at Nuwara Eliya (Suggestions – 5 star - Araliya Green city, 4 star – Araliya Red)

Day 4
Nuwara Eliya > Ella 55 KM | 1Hours and 42 Minutes

If you wish to go by train you can visit Nuwara eliya to Ella by train. You can see a nice view there,

Or else you can go by Car. Here you can visit,

  • Nine Arch bride
  • Little Adam's peak
  • Ravana waterfall

Overnight stay at Ella (Suggestions – 4 star – Hide Ella, 98 Acres )

Day 5
Ella > Bentota 198 KM | 3 Hours and 00 Minutes

Checkout from the hotel & proceed to Bentota. Here you can visit,

  • Turtle hatchery
  • Madhu river boat Safari

Overnight stay at Bentota (Suggestions – 5 star – Sheraton Kosgoda or similar - 4 star Dandus)

Day 6
Bentota

Here you can do ,

  • Water sports (Jet Ski ride, Banana boat ride, etc.)
  • Stay relax at the beach

Overnight stay at Bentota (Suggestions – 5 star – Sheraton kosgoda, 4 star – Pandanus beach hotel)

Day 7
Bentota > Colombo 80KM | 1 Hour and 37Minutes

Here you can visit,

  • City tour of Colombo with Galle face green, Old parliament building, Gangaramaya temple, Shopping at One Galle face shopping mall, Pettah Market

Overnight stay at Colombo (Suggestions 5 star – Cinnamon Grand, 4 star- Radisson Colombo)

Day 8
Colombo > Bandaranayke International Airport 32KM | 0Hours and 40Minutes

Here you can check out from the hotel & you will be transfer to Bandaranayke International Airport according to your flight timings.

End of the tour!!!
