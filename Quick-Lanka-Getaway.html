Quick Lanka Gateway

Tour overview

Discover Sri Lanka in a 4-day tour. Start with the coastal city of Negombo, explore the cultural heritage of Kandy, and immerse yourself in the urban life of Colombo. Highlights include the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, Temple of the Tooth, and Colombo city tour. A short, budget-friendly adventure that offers a taste of Sri Lanka.

Program

Day 1
Pick Up from Bandaranaike International Airport > Negambo 8 KM | 0Hours and 16 Minutes

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, our Guide will warmly welcome you at the airport and you will be brought to your accommodation at Negambo. Negambo is a coastal area.

Here you can visit,

  • Fish Market
  • Angurukaramulla Temple
  • Stay Relax at the beach

Overnight stay @ Negambo (Suggestions 5 star – Jetwing Blue, 4 star- Goldi Sands Hotel)

Day 2
Negambo > Kandy 122 KM | 2Hours and 49 Minutes

Here you can check out from the hotel & proceed to Kandy. On the way to Kandy you can visit,

  • Visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage.
  • Herbal & Spice garden

Proceed to Kandy. Here you can visit,

  • Gem Museum
  • Temple of the tooth relic
  • Cultural dance show

Overnight stay @Kandy (Suggestions – 5 star – Mahaweli reach hotel , 4star – Topaz Hotel )

Day 3
Kandy > Colombo 122 KM | 2Hours and 49 Minutes

Here you can visit,

  • City tour of Colombo with Galle face green, Old parliament building, Gangaramaya temple, Shopping at One Galle face shopping mall, Pettah Market

Overnight stay @ Colombo (Suggestions 5 star – Cinnamon Grand, 4 star- Radisson Colombo)

Day 4
Colombo > Bandaranayke International Airport 32KM | 0Hours and 40Minutes

Here you can check out from the hotel & you will be transfer to Bandaranayke International Airport according to your flight timings.

End of Tour
