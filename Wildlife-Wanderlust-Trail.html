Wildlife Wanderlust Trail

Tour overview

Embark on a 9-day tour of Sri Lanka, starting with the beaches of Negombo, exploring the heritage city of Anuradhapura, the wildlife of Minneriya and Uduwalawa National Parks, the cultural treasures of Kandy, the serene beauty of Nuwara Eliya, and ending with relaxation on the beaches of Bentota. A perfect blend of culture, nature, and relaxation.

Program

Day 1
Negombo

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport you will be brought to your accomodation in Negombo. Relax and go to the beach or let us guide you to the Fish market

Day 2
Anuradhapura

The second day we will head to World Heritage city Anuradhapura. On our way, we make a stopover at Wilpattu National Park for a safari. In Anuradhapura, we can visit a number of cultural attractions: Ruwanwelisay, Sri Maha Bodhi, Abhayagiri Stupa, Mihintale, Twin Ponds, Isurumuniya.

Day 3 & 4
Habarana

On day 3, we will go to the famous Lion’s Rock (Sigiriya), after which you can discover the wildlife of Minneriya National Park during a safari tour.

On day 4, the Cultural village tour and a visit to Polonnaruwa is on the programme. Both nights you will stay at an accomodation in Habarana.

Day 5
Kandy

On your way to Kandy and in Kandy there are a number of highlights to discover, for example: Dambulla cave temple, spices garden, gem museum and the Temple of the Tooth Pick your favorites or see them all!

Day 6
Nuwara Eliya

The next location during your tour is the beautiful city Nuwara Eliya and it’s surroundings. Here, in the Sri Lankan hills you will be able to visit a Tea factory, Gregory Lake and Victoria Park

Day 7
Uduwalawa

In the morning, we bring a visit to the beautiful nature of Horton Plains. In the afternoon we do a safari in Uduwalawa National Park where you stay overnight. Will you spot more than 100 elephants? We’re sure you will!

Day 8 & 9
Bentota

The last two days of your trip will be spend on the beautiful coastline of Bentota. You will visit a turtle hatchery and after that, you can relax and enjoy a cocktail at the beach of Bentota, where you will also stay for the night.
