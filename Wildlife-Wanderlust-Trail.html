Wildlife Wanderlust Trail

Tour overview

Embark on a 9-day tour of Sri Lanka, starting with the beaches of Negombo, exploring the heritage city of Anuradhapura, the wildlife of Minneriya and Uduwalawa National Parks, the cultural treasures of Kandy, the serene beauty of Nuwara Eliya, and ending with relaxation on the beaches of Bentota. A perfect blend of culture, nature, and relaxation.

Itinerary

Day 1
Airport > Negombo | 7.7KM | 17min

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport you will be brought to your accomodation in Negombo. Relax and go to the beach or let us guide you to the Fish market

Overnight stay at negambo ( 4 star Amagi Aria or similar , sentido heritance negombo)

Day 2
Negombo > Anuradhapura | 169KM | 3Hr 28min

The second day we will head to World Heritage city Anuradhapura. On our way, we make a stopover at Wilpattu National Park for a safari. In Anuradhapura, we can visit a number of cultural attractions:

  • Ruwanwelisay
  • Sri Maha Bodhi
  • Abhayagiri Stupa
  • Mihintale
  • Twin Pons
  • Isurumuniya

Overnight stay at anuradhapura ( 4 start Rajarata Hotel or similar )

Day 3
Anuradhapura > Habarana | 58KM | 1Hr 3min

On day 3, you can visit ,

  • Lion's Rock
  • Safari at Minneriya National Park

Overnight stay at Habarana ( 4 star Cinnamon village or similar , 5 star cinnamon lodge or similar )

Day 4
Habarana

On day 4, you can visit ,

  • Cultural Village Tour
  • Polonnaruwa ancient city

Overnight stay at Habarana ( 4 star Cinnamon village or similar , 5 star cinnamon lodge or similar )

Day 5
Habarana > Kandy | 94KM | 2Hr 21min

On your way to Kandy and in Kandy there are a number of highlights to discover, for example:

  • Dambulla cave temple
  • Spices garden
  • Gem museum
  • Temple of the Tooth
Pick your favorites or see them all!

Overnight stay at kandy (5 star Mahaweli reach or similar , 4 star cinnamon citadel)

Day 6
Kandy > Nuwara Eliya | 76KM | 2Hr 22min

The next location during your tour is the beautiful city Nuwara Eliya and it's surroundings. Here, in the Sri Lankan hills you will be able to visit

  • Tea factory
  • Gregory Lake
  • Ramboda waterfall
  • Victoria Park

Overnight stay at Nuwara Eliya ( 5 star Araliya green city 4 star Araliya red)

Day 7
Nuwara Eliya > Udawalawa | 3Hr 34min |

In the morning, you can visit ,

  • Horton Plains

Proceed to udawalawa and

In the afternoon we do a safari in Uduwalawa National Park where you stay overnight. Will you spot more than 100 elephants? We're sure you will!

Overnight stay at Udawalawa (4 star Grand Udawalawa , centuria wild hotel)

Day 8
Udawalawa > Bentota | 182KM | 2Hr 43min

The last day of your trip will be spend on the beautiful coastline of Bentota. You will visit ,

  • Turtle Hatchery

After that, you can relax and enjoy a cocktail at the beach of Bentota, where you will also stay for the night.

Overnight stay at Bentota ( 5 star Sheraton Kosgoda , pandanus beach resort )

Day 9
Bentota > Airport | 108KM | 1Hr 40min

In the morning you can visit ,

  • Boat safari at Madhu river

You can proceed to Airport

End of the Tour
