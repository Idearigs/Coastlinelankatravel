Culture Vista Trek

Tour details

Experience Sri Lanka's beauty in a 5-day tour. Start with the beaches of Negombo, explore the wildlife of Minneriya National Park and the iconic Lion's Rock in Habarana. Discover the cultural treasures of Kandy including the Temple of the Tooth. This tour offers a blend of relaxation, wildlife exploration, and cultural immersion.

Itinerary

Day 1
Airport > Negombo | 7.7KM | 17min

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport you will be brought to your accommodation in Negombo. relax and go to the beach or let us guide you to the Fish market

Overnight stay at Negombo
( 4 star – Amagi aria or similar, 5 star – Sentido Heritance Negambo or similar )

Day 2
Negombo > Habarana | 150KM | 3Hr 5min

On day 2, we first will discover the fascinating wildlife of Minneriya National Park during a safari tour. Secondly, we will bring a visit to a traditional Sri Lankan village.

Overnight stay at Habarana
( - 4 star – Habarana Village by cinnamon or similar, 5 star – Cinnamon Lodge or similar )

Day 3
Habarana

The next day, you can visit ,

  • Lion's Rock
  • Polonnaruwa

Overnight stay at Habarana
( - 4 star – Habarana Village by cinnamon or similar, 5 star – Cinnamon Lodge or similar )

Day 4
Habarana > Kandy | 94KM | 2Hr 36min

Along the way to Kandy and in Kandy, there are a number of highlights to discover, for example ,

  • Dambulla cave temple
  • Spices garden, gem museum
  • Temple of the Tooth
  • Cultural dance
  • Local Market
Pick your favorites or see them all!

Overnight stay at Habarana
( -4 star – Cinnamon Citadel or similar, 5 star – Mahaweli Reach or similar )

Day 5
Kandy > airport | 113KM | 2Hr 39min

Today, we will drop you off at the airport (or at another location).
