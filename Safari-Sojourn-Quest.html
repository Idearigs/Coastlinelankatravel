Safari Sojourn Questt

Tour overview

Experience the diverse beauty of Sri Lanka in an 11-day tour. From the beaches of Negombo, the cultural heritage of Anuradhapura and Kandy, the natural beauty of Nuwara Eliya, to the wildlife of Yala and Sinharaja Rainforest. End your journey relaxing on the beaches of Bentota. A perfect blend of culture, nature, and relaxation awaits you.

Program

Day 1
Negombo

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport you will be brought to your accomodation in Negombo. Relax and go to the beach or let us guide you to the Fish market

Day 2
Anuradhapura

The second day we will head to World Heritage city Anuradhapura. On our way, we make a stopover at Wilpattu National Park for a safari. In Anuradhapura, we can visit a number of cultural attractions: Ruwanwelisay, Sri Maha Bodhi, Abhayagiri Stupa, Mihintale, Twin Ponds, Isurumuniya.

Day 3 & 4
Habarana

On day 3, we will go to the famous Lion’s Rock (Sigiriya), after which you can discover the wildlife of Minneriya National Park during a safari tour.

On day 4, the Cultural village tour and a visit to Polonnaruwa is on the programme. Both nights you will stay at an accomodation in Habarana.

Day 5 & 6
Kandy

On your way to Kandy and in Kandy there are a number of highlights to discover, for example: Dambulla cave temple, spices garden, gem museum, Temple of the Tooth, cultural dance, Peradeniya Botanical Gardens and the local markets. Pick your favorites or see them all in our two days in Kandy!

Day 7
Nuwara Eliya

The next location during your tour is the beautiful city Nuwara Eliya and it’s surroundings. Here, in the Sri Lankan hills you will be able to visit a Tea factory, Gregory Lake and Victoria Park

Day 8
Yala

Have you brought your binoculars? Because, today you have the change to spot the leopard, elephants and numerous other animals during a Safari in Yala National Park.

Day 9
Sinhajara Rainforest

Have you brought your binoculars? Because, today you have the change to spot the leopard, elephants and numerous other animals during a Safari in Yala National Park.On our way to the beach, we bring you to the last piece of tropcial rainforest in Sri Lanka: Sinharaja National Park. The biodiversity in the reserve is enormous, which makes it a treasure trove of endemic species. In the morning you will be waken up by the sounds of the National Park. Shall we take antoher walk? It will be your last Sri Lanka safari during the WIldlife Tour.

Day 10 & 11
Bentota

The last two days of your trip will be spend on the beautiful coastline of Bentota. You will visit a turtle hatchery and after that, you can relax and enjoy a cocktail at the beach of Bentota, where you will also stay for the night.
