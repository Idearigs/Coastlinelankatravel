Safari Sojourn Quest

Tour overview

Experience the diverse beauty of Sri Lanka in an 11-day tour. From the beaches of Negombo, the cultural heritage of Anuradhapura and Kandy, the natural beauty of Nuwara Eliya, to the wildlife of Yala and Sinharaja Rain forest. End your journey relaxing on the beaches of Bentota. A perfect blend of culture, nature, and relaxation awaits you.

Itinerary

Day 1
Airport > Anuradhapura | 168KM | 3Hr 44min

After arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, our Guide will warmly welcome you at the airport and you will be brought to your accommodation at Anuradhapura.

Here you can visit

  • Safari at Wilpattu national park

Overnight stay at ( 4 start Rajarata hotels )

Day 2
Anuradhapura > Sigiriya | 74KM | 1Hr 29min

Before leaving from Anuradhapura you can visit,

  • City tour of Anuradhapura ( Ruwanweliseya, Jaya sri maha bodhiya, Isurumuni royal temple)

Checkout from the hotel & proceed to Sigiriya. In the evening you can visit,

  • Herbal & Ayurvedic Spa

Overnight stay at Sigiriya (Hotels options - 4 star – Habarana Village by cinnamon or similar, 5 star – Cinnamon Lodge or similar)

Day 3
Sigirya

Here you can visit

  • Lion Rock
  • Traditional Village Tour
  • Safari at Minneriya Natioal park

Overnight stay at Sigiriya (Hotels options - 4 star – Habarana Village by cinnamon or similar, 5 star – Cinnamon Lodge or similar)

Day 4
Sigiriya > NuwaraEliya | 199KM | 4Hr 5min

Here you can check out from the hotel and proceed to Nuwara Eliya. On the way to Nuwara Eliya you can visit,

  • Dambulla cave temple
  • Herbal & Spice garden
  • Ramboda Waterfall
  • Tea factory & tea garden

Reach to Nuwara Eliya & check in to hotel. In the evening you can visit,

  • Gregory Lake

Overnight stay at Nuwara Eliya (Hotels options - 4 star – Araliya Red or similar, 5 star – Araliya Green City or similar)

Day 5
NuwaraEliya > Udawalawe | 154KM | 3Hr 58min

Proceed Udawalawa. On the way you can visit,

  • City tour of Ella (Nine arch bridge,Little Adam’s peak , Ella town)

Reach to Udawalawa & check in to hotel

Overnight stay at Udawalawa (Hotels options - Hotels options - 4 star – Centuria Wild / Grand Udawalawe or Similar)

Day 6
Udawalawa > Bentota | 178.9KM | 2Hr 36min

Early in the morning you can visit,

  • Safari at Udawalawa national park
  • Elephant transit home ( Baby elephants feeding session)

Proceed to Bentota. Check in to hotel

  • Stay Relax at the Beach

Overnight stay at Bentota (Hotels options - 4 star – Pandanus beach resort & spa or Similar, 5 star – Sheraton kosgoda or similar)

Day 7
Bentota > Bandaranaike International Airport | 108KM | 1Hr 40min

In the morning you can visit,

  • Turtle hatchery
  • Boat safari at Madu river

Drive to the Airport

End of the tour!
